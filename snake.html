Educational Snake Game

In 2023, me and a group of four others created a snake game based around East Asia geography. The background features a map of East Asia and four dots randomly spawn on the counties. On the top of the screen displays the name of a country and the user has to navigate the snake to one of the dots. If the correct dot is eaten, the user's points increase by one and the four dots switch positions. If an incorrect dot is eaten, the player's lives are decreased by one and no points are gains. The dots still change positions

In this particular project, I worked on the methods which spawned the dots in random locations, checked whether the snake is touching the dot, and the display for the current country on top of the screen.
