1.1.6 MIT App Inventor Project

In 2021-2022 in the class, Computer Science Essentials, I teamed up with Leo Zhu to create an exciting project using MIT App Inventor. Our project, the 'Clicking Game,' is a progressive gaming experience where players deal damage by tapping and earn coins by defeating monsters. As players advance, they unlock more powerful weapons to face tougher challenges. Our collaborative approach involved immediate programming without task division. For detailed information about the game, please refer to documentation.
