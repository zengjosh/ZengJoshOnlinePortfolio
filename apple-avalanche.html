1.2.3 Apple Avalanche CSP 2022

In the school year 2022-2023, within the Advanced Placement Computer Science Principles course, I embarked on the 1.2.3 'Apple Avalanche' project. This project introduced a dynamic typing game wherein users interacted with falling apples by clicking on the letter assigned to each apple.

Our development approach embraced paired programming, where my collaborator, Matthew Loo, and I alternated roles. This cooperative methodology allowed one of us to guide the other, fostering an environment conducive to achieving a mutually satisfactory end result.
