1.2.4 MIT App Inventor Project

In the 2021-2022 school year, during Computer Science Essentials, I undertook the 1.2.4 'SurveySays' project. This project entailed the development of an interactive survey platform, allowing both creators and users to contribute various responses for voting. Subsequently, users could access the survey results, revealing the most popular responses.

My approach to this programming task involved adhering to the Project Lead The Way framework, which provided a structured roadmap for task completion and goal achievement. For comprehensive insights into the project, please refer to the detaileddocumentation.
